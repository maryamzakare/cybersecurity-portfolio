# AI and Cybersecurity Research Presentation

## What This Project Is About

This project was a research presentation about how artificial intelligence connects to cybersecurity in modern life.

I reviewed research papers, compared their ideas, and presented what I learned about AI's role in cyber defense, cyberattacks, privacy, and security risk.

## Why I Made It

AI is changing how people work, communicate, and protect systems. I wanted to understand both sides: how defenders can use AI and how attackers may misuse it.

## What I Worked On

- Found cybersecurity research papers related to AI
- Reviewed the main ideas and findings
- Compared different research perspectives
- Connected AI concepts to real-world cybersecurity
- Presented the topic to others

## Topics I Studied

- AI-assisted threat detection
- Machine learning in cybersecurity
- AI and phishing
- AI-assisted social engineering
- Privacy risks
- Adversarial AI
- Human decision-making with AI tools

## Skills I Practiced

- Research review
- Cybersecurity analysis
- Technical reading
- Presentation
- Public speaking
- Critical thinking
- Professional communication

## What I Learned

I learned that AI can support cybersecurity, but it also creates new risks.

AI tools can help defenders analyze data and detect threats, but attackers can also use AI to make attacks faster, more convincing, or harder to detect.
